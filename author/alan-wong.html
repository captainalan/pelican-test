Desk jobs

Knowing how to work a bunch of little programs is like knowing how to cast a bunch of spells. Chaining them together to create solutions, now that's wizardry! Composing emails is basically mind control ("delegation").

Magical items (computers) become more powerful in the hands of skilled users. Understanding that when you skillfully operate a computer, you are translating keystrokes (little finger movements) into BIG ACTIONS in the real world.

Practicing a musical instrument
