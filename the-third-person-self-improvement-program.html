The Third-Person Self Improvement Program

Published: Sun 22 March 2020
By Alan Wong

In Lifestyle.

Imagine yourself from a third person perspective. That is, imagine watching yourself from someone else's viewpoint. What is this person like? Are they acting in a self-destructive way? Are they good to others? Are they idealistic but utterly incompetent? How would you describe yourself in a novel?
